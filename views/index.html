nusmodchat

A community-maintained portal to view all available telegram chats for NUS modules.

Listing

    Publish

    If you cannot find the module in the listing, you may create one and publish it here. Just follow these 4 simple steps:

    Convert your group to a supergroup.

    Rename the group to be in the form of "CS1231 Discussion".

    Add @NUSModchatBot into the chat as an administrator.

    Execute the /start command.

    Contact @nexolute for any enquiry.
